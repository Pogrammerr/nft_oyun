Rentstakers is a staking game with fabulous 3D art of 20,000 (Gen0 & Gen1) unique Apartment & Residence NFTs on Avalanche Chain. In order to join us, you must mint a house (apartment or residence) and stake it on our city. Houses are ERC721 NFTs and produce $RENT every minute when staked. You can exchange the $RENTs you earn from your houses at Traderjoe.

There are also different items (upgrade items, bank, investment pool etc.) in the game. As time passes in the game, players can use the diversity of game to find the most successful strategy. Find the better strategy and become the richest one in the city.

If you want to get more information and be the first to know about the developments, follow our social media accounts.
